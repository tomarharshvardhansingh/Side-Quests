# Side-Quests
Documenting projects, experiments, and ideas I build while learning by doing.

Things I build outside my main learning path — hardware, experiments, and real-world problem solving.

---

## 🧠 Why this repo exists

This is where I document projects that don’t fit into structured learning like DSA or courses.

These are things I actually build, break, fix, and learn from.

Instead of just focusing on theory, this repo is about:
- trying ideas in real life  
- dealing with things not working  
- figuring out solutions step by step  

---

## ⚙️ What you'll find here

- Hardware projects (Arduino, sensors, motors, etc.)
- DIY builds (like custom setups or reused systems)
- Experimental ideas and prototypes
- Things that may or may not work
